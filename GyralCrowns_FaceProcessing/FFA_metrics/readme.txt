The following files contain multimodal metrics of four FFAs (lh_pFus,lh_mFus,rh_pFus,rh_mFus) for 32 subjects specified by Fusiform_sulcal_stats_7.9.19.csv

FFA_myelin.csv: myelination

FFA_thickness.csv: cortical thickness

FFA_activ.csv: face-selective activation (z-stat) from Working Memory task

FFA_activ-emo.csv: face-selective activation (z-stat) from Emotion task

FFA_activ-emo_clean-CNR.csv: face-selective activation (z-stat) from Emotion task (regress out the temporal contrast-to-noise-ratio (CNR)).

rsfc_FFA2MMP.mat: RSFC from FFA to 358 HCP MMP areas

rsfc_FFA2Cole.mat: RSFC from FFA to 12 resting-state networks (RSNs). (average the RSFCs to all 360 HCP MMP areas into the 12-dimension RSFC fingerprint according to the Cole-Anticevic Brain Network Parcellation (CAB-NP) (Ji et al., 2019))

rsfc_FFA2Cole-mean.csv: global brain connectivity (average RSFCs across the 12 RSNs)

rsfc_FFA2Cole-mean_clean-CNR.csv: global brain connectivity after regressing out the CNR
